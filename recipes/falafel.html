Falefel

Description

Place the chickpea grits in a container, cover with water and soak for at least 12 hours and up to 24 hours.

Rinse the soaked grains and grind in a food processor with the cilantro, parsley, garlic and onion until you obtain a uniform puree.

Transfer to a bowl and add the remaining ingredients except the oil.

Mix and let stand for 20 minutes.

Form small balls from the mixture with your hands and set aside.

Heat oil for deep frying and fry the balls until golden brown. Remove to paper towels and serve immediately.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried chickpeas
  • 5 coriander stalks
  • 6 parsley stalks
  • 2 medium onions, peeled and quartered
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Half a teaspoon of baking soda
  • oil for frying
